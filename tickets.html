Tickets

Iron Ticket - 100€

Dive into a day of electrifying sounds with the Iron Ticket. This pass grants access to the heart of Harmony Haven for one unforgettable day of RockFusion Festival. Choose the date that suits you best, and immerse yourself in the sonic symphony, surrounded by stunning landscapes and the pulsating energy of legendary rock performances.

Gold Ticket - 185€

Elevate your experience with the Gold Ticket, offering access to the festival for two days of your choice. Immerse yourself in the dynamic rhythms and anthems that define the rock genre. Whether you're drawn to the legendary classics or eager to discover new favorites, this ticket lets you curate your festival experience across two days at Harmony Haven.

Diamond Ticket - 275€

The ultimate RockFusion experience awaits with the Diamond Ticket. Enjoy unrestricted access for all three days of the festival, allowing you to soak in the full spectrum of rock magic. Immerse yourself in the vibrant community, the breathtaking venue, and the unbridled passion of three days filled with legendary performances.
